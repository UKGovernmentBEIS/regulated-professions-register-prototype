Check a regulated profession in the UK

Some professions in the UK are regulated. If you want to work in a regulated profession, you need to contact the regulatory authority who will recognise your qualification and register you.

Use this service to:

  • Check if your profession is regulated and what qualifications are required
  • Find regulatory authority contact details for your profession
  • View annual figures regarding applications to work in regulated professions in the UK
This service is also available in Welsh.
Start now

Other ways to find information

If you need support checking a regulated profession you can contact The UK Centre for Professional Qualifications (UK CPQ).
