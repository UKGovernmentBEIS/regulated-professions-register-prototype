Work in a regulated profession in the UK

Some professions, jobs and trades in the UK are ‘regulated’. This means you must have certain qualifications or experience to work in them.

If you want to work in a regulated profession in the UK, you need to contact the regulatory authority for it.

The regulatory authority sets the requirements for qualifying for your profession, so it will check your experience and qualifications. If they’re recognised, the regulatory authority will register you.

Use this service to:

  • check if your profession is regulated
  • find contact details for your profession’s regulatory authority
  • view annual figures for applications to work in regulated professions in the UK

You can check if a profession is regulated and the qualifications and experience you need to work in the UK.

Find contact details for regulatory authorities

You can contact the regulator for your profession to find out about the process for becoming a recognised and registered professional.

You can find information about the total number of applications and the number of successful applications for each profession.

Get help checking a regulated profession

Contact the UK Centre for Professional Qualifications (UK CPQ) If you need support checking a regulated profession.
